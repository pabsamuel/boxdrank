# Creator Acceptable Use Policy — DRAFT

> **DRAFT — requires qualified legal review.**

Prohibited in uploaded/imported content: content you don't have rights to (including other creators' emotes and protected characters); hate speech or harassment symbols; sexual content involving minors (reported to authorities), non-consensual sexual content; content sexualizing real persons without consent; graphic violence or gore intended to shock; deceptive impersonation of people or brands (parody must be labeled); malware or exploit payloads in any form; content illegal in [JURISDICTION]. Enforcement ladder: warning → content removal → pack suspension → account termination (repeat infringers terminated per DMCA policy). Appeals via [CONTACT] within 30 days; decisions and reasons are logged (`admin_actions`).
