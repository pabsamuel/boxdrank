## Description

**Your Shopify data, living in Notion — automatically.**

NotionSync keeps Notion databases of your orders and products that update
themselves. Connect a Notion integration, click "Create databases for me",
and from then on every new order, order update, cancellation, product
change and product delete lands in Notion within seconds — no exports, no
CSVs, no Zapier chains.

**Set up in three steps**
1. Paste a Notion integration secret (stored encrypted, never shared)
2. Pick a page — NotionSync creates ready-made Orders and Products databases
3. Run the initial sync: the last 90 days of orders and your full catalog

**Built to be trusted**
- No duplicates: updates modify the existing Notion page; deleted products
  are archived, and rows you remove by hand are recreated on next change
- Notion rate limits respected automatically; failed writes retry
- A status page shows exactly what synced, when, and any errors
- Your data goes only to YOUR Notion workspace — we don't store order contents

**Who it's for**
Teams who run their business in Notion, agencies building client wikis and
dashboards, and anyone who wants store data next to their docs and plans.
